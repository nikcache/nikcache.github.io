Local Interaction Model Simulator

  • Collaborated with 2 peers to develop a python application to simulate Local Interaction Models (Eshel et al., 1998).
  • Traversed over 4,000+ nodes and 16,000+ links with efficient algorithms to minimize runtime less than 10s/10 units of time.
  • Incorporated inputs for custom weights and parameters within the models to study variability of results w.r.t. parameters.
